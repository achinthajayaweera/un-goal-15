About This Project

Created for the Web Design and Development module at the Informatics Institute of Technology (IIT), Sri Lanka — in partnership with the University of Westminster.

The project focuses on UN Sustainable Development Goal 15 — Life on Land — educating the public about terrestrial ecosystem conservation and inspiring action.

About This Project

This website was created as part of the Web Design and Development module at the Informatics Institute of Technology (IIT), Sri Lanka — in partnership with the University of Westminster.

The project focuses on UN Sustainable Development Goal 15 — Life on Land — with the aim of educating the public about terrestrial ecosystem conservation and encouraging action.
